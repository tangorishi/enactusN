Problem Statement

Global plastic waste accumulation is a pressing concern, impacting our environment and health. Seven major types of plastic, including PET, HDPE, and dangerous polycarbonates, contribute to this issue. Unfortunately, not all plastics are easily recyclable, with only PET and HDPE commonly accepted. We urgently need alternative solutions like plastic upcycling to address less recyclable plastics and promote environmental sustainability.

Project Overview

Segregated into three holistic threads locked together, the project "PARIVARTANA" comes together as a whole with an expanded entourage as our backbone. Our project focuses mainly on transforming plastic waste into sustainable, high-quality products. Project Parivartana is more than just an initiative; it represents a movement towards a cleaner and greener future. Through its holistic approach, ‘Parivartana’ seeks to inspire lasting change and set a new paradigm for plastic waste management, forging a path towards a more sustainable and environmentally conscious society.

Project Details

We begin with plastic waste collection by deploying trash bins at places with heavy foot traffic. The second tangent runs along recycling and product manufacturing, particularly products such as tote bags, laundry bags and floor mats. Our project's final arc aims to reach consumers at the very root level by spreading awareness through collaborations with academic institutions and hosting seminars on recycling plastic. We also offer Special DIY (Do-it-yourself) kits to schools aiming to teach students about sustainable product usage and recycling at a tender age. Bringing into the limelight the selfless contributions of individuals that go unrecognized is another important part of our project. Our project addresses Goal 12 and 13 of the UN SDG, which is ‘Responsible Consumption and Production’ and ‘Climate action’ respectively.

Key Activities

Our comprehensive approach to tackling plastic pollution involves hosting seminars, workshops, and educational initiatives to raise awareness, promote waste collection, and emphasize recycling. We aim to incentivize waste collection with user-friendly trash bins while prioritizing sustainability through recycling and eco-friendly product manufacturing. Additionally, we're committed to educating the next generation about plastic's harmful impacts through DIY kits and organizing events to empower participants with the knowledge and skills needed for effective waste management.

MISSION AND VISION

Mission fuels purpose, while vision paints our destiny with clarity.

Mission

Educate, repurpose plastic waste, promote responsible segregation.

Our mission is to create high-quality sustainable products catering to the market demand using innovative plastic upcycling techniques while focusing our attention on not-easily recyclable plastic.

Vision

Create sustainable products from challenging non-recyclable plastic.

We plan to reach out to a larger audience by spreading awareness and educating about the dire consequences of plastic waste to future humankind. Through this, we envision a future with reduced plastic pollution through plastic waste being repurposed into daily usage products and a responsible waste segregation model implemented in every household.
